  • What is Picha Project?

    The Picha Project is a Impact Driven Enterprise that empowers marginalised groups in Malaysia through a sustainable food delivery and catering business.

    We deliver and cater authentic traditional meals from different cultures prepared by refugee families from various countries to companies, events and organisations.

    Who are Picha Ninja?

    Picha Ninjas assists Picha to deliver food on time to our customers.
    In The Picha Project, we take our food delivery seriously and if you are interested to earn some extra income while volunteering to rebuild lives and make an impact in the refugee community together, let us know.
    Our food delivery starts from the refugee family's house to our customers, it's just that simple. Do note that you have to be VERY on time as we promise timeliness to our customer.
